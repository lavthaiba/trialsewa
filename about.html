About Us

At TRIALSEWA, we are committed to revolutionizing the vehicle trial booking experience. Our mission is to provide a seamless and convenient platform for booking car, bike, and scooter trials. We understand the challenges faced by vehicle enthusiasts and aim to simplify the process through our innovative online service.

We are dedicated to making vehicle trials accessible and hassle-free. By leveraging digital technology, we are setting a new standard in the trial sector in Nepal. Our vision extends beyond borders, as we plan to expand our services globally and collaborate with businesses to offer corporate training programs for driving lessons.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between trial centers and vehicle enthusiasts by offering a user-friendly online booking platform. We aim to provide exceptional customer service, streamline the booking process, and offer a range of vehicles to suit diverse needs.

Our Vision

We envision transforming the vehicle trial sector by introducing innovative solutions and expanding our reach worldwide. We aspire to collaborate with businesses, offering tailored corporate training and enhancing the driving experience for all.

About TRIALSEWA

TRIALSEWA is a platform that simplifies booking vehicle trials at local centers, providing a seamless experience for users. We act as a bridge between you and the trial centers, making the process hassle-free.
